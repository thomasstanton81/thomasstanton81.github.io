Different Tags

  • head- mainly a title or a navigation bar the head appears at the top of a webpage
  • body- where all the main information is. It is under the head and will be the largest part of a webpage
  • p- the p lets you create a paragraph and write text in it.
  • h1,2,3 - a heading tag to help break up a page into different sections. can be used multiple times as well (h1,h2,h3 etc)
  • footer- will appear at the end or bottom of a web page.
